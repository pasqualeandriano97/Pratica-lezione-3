Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,Cranberries, and brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized inions,tart cranberries,and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

Fresh Caranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil,plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  1. 3 medium yellow onions,thinly sliced
  2. 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegard
  3. 1-2 tablespoons olive oil,for brushing on top
  4. 10 ounces brie,cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife in desired,thought it's not necessary)
  5. 1 1/2 cups fresh of frozen cranberries

Method

1 Make the focaccia dough

In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mizer with a paddle attachment,mix togheder all the dought ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dought,thought i sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the las of the flour...
